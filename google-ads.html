Why Google Ads for Law Firms?

Legal keywords cost $20–$100+ per click — some personal injury terms exceed $200. At those prices, a poorly managed campaign can burn through thousands in days with nothing to show for it. Our team specializes in legal advertising, so every dollar is strategically placed to attract potential clients who are actively looking for an attorney right now.

Unlike general marketing agencies, we understand legal ethics rules, bar advertising guidelines, and the nuances of marketing different practice areas. We build campaigns that are compliant, competitive, and focused on one metric that matters: signed cases.

Ad Types We Manage

Search Ads

Text ads placed at the very top of Google when someone searches for terms like "car accident lawyer near me" or "best divorce attorney." We target high-intent keywords specific to your practice areas and geography, so you only pay for clicks from people ready to hire.

Local Service Ads

Appear above regular search results with Google's "Google Guaranteed" badge, your star rating, and a direct call button. You only pay per lead — not per click. Ideal for firms targeting clients in a specific city or region.

YouTube Ads

Build trust before a client even calls. Short, targeted video ads introduce your firm to people researching legal topics on YouTube. Great for brand awareness and establishing authority in your practice area.

Display & Remarketing

97% of visitors leave a website without converting on the first visit. Our remarketing campaigns follow those visitors across the web with banner ads, keeping your firm top of mind until they're ready to reach out.

Our Process

We start with a deep-dive audit of your current advertising, competitors, and market. From there we build a custom campaign strategy, write compelling ad copy, design landing pages optimized for conversions, and continuously monitor and optimize performance. You'll receive transparent monthly reports showing exactly how your budget is performing — leads generated, cost per lead, and cases signed.
